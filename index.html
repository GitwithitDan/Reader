How to use Reader

Opening

Tap Open Reader and allow camera and microphone. The app starts listening right away — no extra steps.

Scanning a document

  • Point your phone at the document
  • Tap the white circle button, or say "read this"
  • Reader will tell you if anything needs adjusting (lighting, framing)
  • On success, a summary appears and Reader speaks a brief overview

Asking questions

Just talk. Reader is always listening. Ask anything about the document and it will answer aloud.

Voice commands

  • Read this / Scan this / Capture — scan the document
  • Summarize — brief overview
  • Read it to me — read full text aloud
  • Next / previous paragraph — navigate paragraph by paragraph
  • Next / previous section — jump between headings or sections
  • Next / previous page — navigate between captured pages
  • Repeat — hear the last response again
  • Show document — view full captured text on screen
  • Clear / new document — start fresh

Multiple pages

  • Say "Multiple pages" to start multi-page mode
  • Capture each page in sequence
  • Say "Done" when finished — Reader will treat them as one document

Low light & tips

  • Works in low light as long as text is legible
  • Hold steady for a moment before tapping capture
  • Tap anywhere on screen to stop Reader mid-sentence
